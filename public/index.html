Who We Are & Why We Built This

TrioTech is a team of passionate developers committed to creating innovative web solutions. The ChatCord app is designed to facilitate seamless communication, enabling users to share knowledge and engage in discussions across various topics. Our goal is to connect communities through technology.

Our Technology Stack

We leverage MongoDB for flexible data storage, React for a dynamic and responsive front-end, and Node.js to handle server-side logic efficiently. This combination ensures a robust and scalable real-time chat application.
